Meet The Demoman

Real Name: Tavish Finnegan DeGroot
Age: ~30
Residence: Ullapool, Scotland

General Tips

This drunken demolitions expert hails from Scotland, and lost his FIRST set of adoptive parents at the age of 6 in his attempt to blow the Loch Ness Monster to smithereens. He has a pipe bomb launcher that deals massive damage if you can hit it dead on. He also has stickybombs he can rig around corners and on the other side of entrances vital for the enemy.
